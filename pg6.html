So what can YOU do to help yourself?

The good thing is that knowing about this issue is half of the solution. The other half comes from taking the initiative to learn more about your computer and what it can do for you.

Keeping your computer safe from viruses is vital, yet how many actually know what one looks like?

The best resource for information on computers is the internet, and though doesnt take any technological profecciency to use Google to find a solution for each problem as it arises, this really doesn't help anyone learn the skills to avoid the problems in the first place.
It's more important to try to find how the problem can be prevented, and what causes it in the first place.
