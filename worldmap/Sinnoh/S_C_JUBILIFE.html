Jubilife City is a massive city in the south-west of Sinnoh. It features a variety of areas key to the game. First, the Global Trade Station is located in the south-west part of the city allowing you to trade Pokémon and do a variety of WiFi features. Near the top of the city is the Pokétch company, where you will be sent through the city looking for clowns in order to earn your very own Pokétch. Finally, there is a TV station and a Trainer School where a variety of trainers dwell. In the TV station, there is a daily lottery based upon Pokémon ID numbers where you can win a variety of prizes including the Master Ball
