Route 202 Trainers Pokemon Items
North Exit : Jubilife City
South Exit : Sandgem Town

Route 202 is a north-facing route between Sandgem Town and Jubilife City. This route is a fairly short route which zigzags when going north. It has a few trainers within it who shall challenge you but contains nothing else noteworthy.

Fancy Plat
Makuhita, which often carries a macho brace, can be found on this route. Take this opportunity to boost your stat gain early!
Blastoise is very common here. Not only is it a strong Pokemon, but it can sometimes hold the TM for Ice Beam, a very useful move that Blastoise can learn!

Michaux Challenge
There is not much to note here, however swinub can become a decently strong Pokemon after time.
